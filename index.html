3D Solar System

3D rendering of planets involves creating realistic visual models of celestial bodies using computer graphics. It simulates textures, lighting, and atmospheric effects to depict planets with scientific accuracy or artistic style.

ROTOSCOPE

1-minute Video Rotoscope

Video rotoscoping is a technique where artists trace over footage frame by frame to isolate subjects or create visual effects. It's commonly used for background removal, motion tracking, and stylized animation.
